Ayaz

Ayabulela Ayerz Mabinda was born in Cape Town, Kwalanga Sec 21. Late 1990s moved to the Eastern Cape and began rapping at the age of 13, 2004. The music has always ran within the family blood as my father was a lead singer of a Sicathamiya Band in his community. Ayerz Mabinda is a second born child in a family of five children. When you listen to Ayerz Mabinda Music you get to hear about all his life.
